How to use it

Three taps to a cheaper tank.

01

Search your suburb

Type your suburb, or tap “Use my location”. We rank the closest stations and show the cheapest one near you, by fuel type.

02

Pick the right day

Switch between Today and Tomorrow — FuelWatch posts tomorrow's prices at 2:30pm. Open History to see your suburb's daily prices and spot the weekly low.

03

Drive there

Tap the arrow on any station to navigate straight there in Google Maps, or tap the bell to get a heads-up when its price hits the bottom of the cycle.
